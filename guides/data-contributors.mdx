## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  1. **Install Chrome Extension**
     Visit the [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore) and install the Sera extension

  2. **Connect Wallet**
     Click the Sera icon and connect your preferred crypto wallet

  3. **Configure Settings**
     Set your privacy preferences and data sharing controls

  4. **Start Contributing**
     Begin interacting with supported AI models - your contributions are automatically recorded
</Steps>

## Maximizing Rewards

### Reward Formula Explained
$$
R = α · Q(c) · V(s) · e^{-λt} · (1 + ΣβᵢMᵢ)
$$

Where:
- $α$ = Base rate
- $Q(c)$ = Quality score
- $V(s)$ = Value score
- $λt$ = Time decay
- $βᵢMᵢ$ = Model-specific multipliers

<Note>
Quality and uniqueness are key factors in determining rewards. Higher-quality contributions receive significantly better rewards.
</Note>
