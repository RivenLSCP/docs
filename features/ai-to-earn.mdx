**AI-To-Earn** revolutionizes data collection by enabling users to monetize their AI interactions while contributing to the advancement of artificial intelligence. Built natively on the SUI blockchain, our protocol transforms AI interactions into valuable digital assets, creating a decentralized ecosystem where contributors earn $SERA tokens for quality AI conversations and data contributions.


## How It Works

<Steps>
  1. **Connect Wallet**  
     Link your SUI wallet to start earning. Supported wallets include:
     - Sui Wallet
     - OKX Wallet
     - Other SUI-compatible wallets

  2. **Start Contributing**  
     Engage with supported AI models. Your interactions are securely recorded on-chain as verifiable contributions.

  3. **Earn SERA**  
     Receive $SERA tokens based on your contribution quality, automatically distributed through smart contracts.

  4. **Boost Earnings**  
     Stake $SERA tokens to increase your earning multiplier and unlock premium features.
</Steps>

## Reward Formula


  $$
  R = \alpha \cdot Q(c) \cdot V(s) \cdot e^{-\lambda t} \cdot \left(1 + \sum_{i=1}^{n} \beta_i M_i\right)
  $$

  Where:
  - $α$: Base rate
  - $Q(c)$: Quality Score (engagement & consistency)
  - $V(s)$: Value Score (uniqueness & significance)
  - $λt$: Time decay factor
  - $βᵢMᵢ$: Model-specific multipliers


#### Quality Score $Q(c)$

$$
Q(c) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-k(q - q_0)}} \cdot \sqrt{\frac{\sigma^2}{\sigma^2 + \sigma_0^2}}
$$

- **Description**: Evaluates conversation quality using engagement metrics and consistency measures.

#### Value Score $V(s)$

$$
V(s) = \log(1 + \frac{s}{s_0}) \cdot (1 + \gamma \cdot \text{unique}(s))
$$

- **Description**: Calculates content value based on uniqueness and significance factors.
