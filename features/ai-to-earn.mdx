**AI-To-Earn** enables users to monetize their AI model interactions while contributing to AI research advancement. Built natively on the SUI blockchain with Walrus storage integration, the protocol securely captures and stores your AI conversations while rewarding quality contributions with $SERA tokens.


## How It Works

<Steps>
  1. **Connect Wallet**  
     Connect your SUI-compatible wallet (e.g., Sui Wallet, OKX Wallet). This wallet manages your $SERA tokens and signs your interactions.

  2. **Install Chrome Extension**  
     Our browser extension captures your conversations with supported AI models, encrypts sensitive data, and generates content hashes.

  3. **Secure Storage**  
     Conversation data is split and encoded using Walrus's Red Stuff protocol, ensuring:
     - Data redundancy across storage nodes
     - Fast retrieval speeds
     - Byzantine fault tolerance
     - Privacy through encryption

  4. **Earn SERA**  
     - Conversations are quality-checked through our off-chain oracle network
     - Quality scores and metadata are published on-chain
     - Smart contracts distribute $SERA rewards based on contribution value
</Steps>

## Reward System

Contributions are evaluated through a combination of off-chain oracles and on-chain verification:
  $$
R = α · Q(c) · M(t) · V(i) · (1 + B)
$$

Where:
- $α$ (alpha): Base rate defined in smart contract
- $Q(c)$: Quality score (0.1 to 1.0)
- $M(t)$: Model tier multiplier (1.0x - 1.5x)
- $V(i)$: Volume multiplier (1.0x - 1.5x)
- $B$: Bonus multipliers (referrals, streaks)

## Privacy & Security Architecture

### Data Protection Flow

<Steps>
  1. **Local Encryption**
     - Personal data encrypted in Chrome extension
     - Uses AES-256 encryption
     - Keys never leave user device

  2. **Walrus Storage**
     - Data split using Red Stuff encoding
     - Encrypted slivers distributed across nodes
     - Redundancy ensures 99.9% availability

  3. **On-Chain Records**
     - Only hashes and metadata stored on SUI
     - No personal data on-chain
     - Pseudonymous wallet addresses
</Steps>
