## Installation

### Chrome Extension

1. Visit the [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore)
2. Search for "Sera GPT-To-Earn"
3. Click "Add to Chrome"
4. Follow the setup wizard to connect your wallet

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="wallet" title="Connect Your Wallet">
    Sera requires a crypto wallet for receiving rewards and participating in the marketplace. We support:
    - MetaMask
    - WalletConnect
    - Sui Wallet
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="message" title="Start Contributing Data">
    1. Open ChatGPT
    2. Start your conversations
    3. The Sera extension will automatically track eligible conversations
    4. Earn points based on quality and volume
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="coins" title="Earn Rewards">
    - Basic rewards = T * S ^q
    - T: Data Volume Coefficient
    - S: Volume per session
    - q: Quality Multiplier
    - Referral bonuses available
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
