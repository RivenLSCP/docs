## Installation

### Browser Extension

1. Visit the [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore)
2. Search for "Sera AI-To-Earn"
3. Click "Add to Chrome"
4. Follow the setup wizard to connect your wallet

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="wallet" title="Connect Your Wallet">
    Sera requires a crypto wallet for receiving rewards and participating in the marketplace. We support:
    - MetaMask
    - WalletConnect
    - Sui Wallet
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="message" title="Start Contributing Data">
    1. Open your preferred AI platforms
    2. Start your interactions
    3. The Sera extension will automatically track eligible AI interactions
    4. Earn points based on quality and contribution value
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="coins" title="Earn Rewards">
    - Base Formula: $$R = α·Q(c)·V(s)·e^(-λt)·(1 + ΣβᵢMᵢ)$$
    - Q(c): Quality Score (engagement & consistency)
    - V(s): Value Score (uniqueness & significance)
    - M: Model-specific multipliers (1.0-2.0x)
    - Additional bonuses through referrals & quests
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
